Work

iSeeVC

Webcam AI• July 2019 - November 2019

While working for the video conferencing service, iSeeVC, during my work integrated learning project at the University of Newcastle, I researched and made some progress on developing real-time background removal software that could operate without the use of a green screen. To do this I used Tensorflow's open source model DeepLab-v3+ to remove the background from each frame of the user's webcam. I sought to then feed the resulting video into the iSeeVC's Unity powered virtual environment in real-time. Unfortunately I was not able to get the video segmentation to perform at the minimum speed for use within the video conferencing software. I must admit I was well over my head, working with some of the most cutting edge software from Google's leading deep learning engineers. Despite this I benefited from this exposure to new technologies by acquiring an increased awareness of machine learning models and techniques in the Tensorflow libraries. I look forward to working on more ambitious machine learning and data centric projects such as this one in the future.
